Our Story

Jyothi Bharathi Adarsha College (JBAC) was established in 1972 with a vision to provide quality higher education to students from all walks of life in Hyderabad and the surrounding region.

Over five decades, JBAC has grown from a small junior college to a full-fledged degree college offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programmes in Arts, Science, Commerce, and Computer Applications.

The college is affiliated to Osmania University, Hyderabad, and has been accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC. With a sprawling 10-acre campus in Bandlaguda Jagir, JBAC offers world-class facilities in a green, serene environment.

Vision

Mission

  • Promote research, innovation, and critical thinking.
  • Instil values of integrity, discipline, and community service.
  • Develop industry-ready graduates through skills training and internships.
