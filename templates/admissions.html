Admission Process

  • UG Programmes: Passed 10+2 (Intermediate) from a recognised board with minimum 45% aggregate marks.
  • PG Programmes: Passed relevant UG degree with minimum 50% aggregate marks.
  • MBA: Any UG degree with minimum 50% marks. Entrance test required.
  • SC/ST/BC candidates: 5% relaxation in minimum marks.

  • 10th class marks memo (SSC)
  • Intermediate / UG marks memo
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from last institution
  • Caste Certificate (for reserved category)
  • Aadhaar Card / Photo ID
  • Income Certificate (for fee concession)
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Migration Certificate (for students from other universities)

  1. Collect the application form from the college office or download it from the website.
  2. Fill the form completely and attach all required documents.
  3. Submit the form along with the application fee at the college counter.
  4. Attend the counselling session as per the schedule.
  5. Pay the tuition and other fees after seat allotment.
